Door Systems — Lift / Slide (Conventional)

When it comes to ease of operation and space efficiency, nothing matches the versatility and attractiveness of our Sliding Door Systems. And any of our glass doors can be transformed to create this sleek and modern design that's ideal for patios and garden areas. Choose from our Magnum or Lift / Slide Sliding Door Systems in a conventional or pocketing application, dependent upon your specific climate and space needs.

 

For the ultimate in weather-tight performance, security and ease of operation, this sliding door system utilizes European hardware to lower the door onto the sill when in the closed position, creating a weather-tight seal, unmatched by traditional sliding systems. Available in all of our glass door styles with individual door panels up to 4' wide and 10' tall, doors must be 2¼" thick. Ships complete with all jambs, screen doors, weather-stripping and hardware.
