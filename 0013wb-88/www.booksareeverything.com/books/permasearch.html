PERMA
Perma Books were published by Doubleday and Company and produced two lines of books: one hardbound and one paperbound. A number of non-fiction titles were published alongside other popular literature. They published two books on magic, both highly collectible. Pocket Books purchased this line in 1954, and continued it using the Pocket format. A number of collectible mysteries were published including authors such as: Ian Fleming, Henry Kuttner and Ed McBain.
