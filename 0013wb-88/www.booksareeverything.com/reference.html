Putting Dell on the Map: A History of the Dell Paperbacks by William H. Lyles

First Edition 1983; Hardback, No Dust Jacket as Issued, Fine Condition (Unread Copy)

A detailed history of Dell Books and Western Printing, beginning with their first book in 1942 through 1962. This edition lists 2,168 Dell paperbacks and 577 back cover maps, many of which are pictured. Long out of print and one of the rarest reference books.
