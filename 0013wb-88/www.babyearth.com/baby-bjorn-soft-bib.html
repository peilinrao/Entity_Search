Features

The BABY BJÖRN Soft Bib is ergonomically designed to fit a child's body. The neck is designed like a necklace with continuous fastening, so it can be adjusted to fit your child perfectly.

Feaures:

  • Deep pocket to catch food spills
  • Practical hole for hanging when not in use
  • Soft beads around the neck make the bib extra comfy
  • Easy, adjustable opening for a growing baby
  • Extra soft, PVC-free material
  • Approved for food use
  • Dishwasher safe
  • Easy to rinse clean

All BabyBjörn product only contain materials that have been thoroughly tested. All materials that will come into contact with food are food-safety approved. BabyBjörn does not use chemical additives such as cadmium, lead, bisphenol A (BPA), phthalates, bromine or chlorine. In addition, their plastics are also PVC free and recyclable!

Mommy in Texas from Houston, TX              April 2nd, 2008
This is the perfect size, and very easy to clean and wipe dry (the "easy to wipe dry" feature is important to us because we need to use the bib many times during the day). The bib is stiff so that the pocket can really catch crumbs, fluid...etc. It's shaped so that it's still very comfortable for the baby (she's 11 months old), and the neckline is beaded with softer rubber type material.
However, because it is stiff, we can't easily carry this with us when we go out. But then this wasn't a surprise for us, as we purposely purchased this stiff bib for use at home so that it can catch crumbs better.
When we go out, we like using the Dura-bib Stage 1, which catches everything very well too, and it can be rolled up so that it's easy to take out with us. But we prefer the Baby Bjorn for frequent home use, as it is easier to clean and wipe dry.
