Super-simplified, basic chopper wiring diagram -
un-complicates things by leaving out all the crap that
real choppers don't need any way: turn signals,
speedometers, radios, horns, bells, whistles, etc.
Diagram Features/Covers:
- Circuit Breakers, starter relay, starter, alternator, ignition,
regulator/rectifier, kill switch/lock, headlight, taillight, brake
lights, battery, etc. - only the bare essentials.
- Let's you get wired fast so you can make the big run, yet
serves as a basis for later add-ons (not covered).
- Color-coded schematics to make drawings easy to read and
wires easy to trace + colors match stock (where possible) to
make matching up to electrical components (such as
headlights/taillights) simpler.
- Designed for ground-up (no harness) chopper wiring to save
you big buck$ (since you don't need to buy a harness with tons
of wire and fixtures you don't need).
- Includes separate basic instructions and pointers sheet.
- Complete tech support via phone and email.
