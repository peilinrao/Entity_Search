[Richtlijnen voor opmerkingen] Opmerking van de fotograaf
Lisbon (old part) March 2009 early morning.

At this hour the city is usual desert of people or cars, and shows with undisguised pride its colours and that special bright light that gave her the name of “white city” (movie of Wim Wenders).

Lisbon is a city very easy to love, by its likeable people, by its light, by the colourful houses that border the narrow streets, full of history, and that come down from its 7 hills looking for the river Tejo.

Lisbon got passionate itself for the river Tejo since the beginning, there grew, there it was, and there it remains since more than 800 years… waiting for you with her perfume and the warm breathing of a quiet and relaxed time.

And if you look for me you must use «the eyes of soul», then, maybe (and I say maybe) you can find me seat in a sun ray.

T.
