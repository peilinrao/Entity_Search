Trailblazers Adventure Program - Greenwich, CT
WhiteTail Solutions - Trailblazers Event Greenwich, CT
On May 6, 2006 the second ever Trailblazer Program in CT.  
Trailblazers is a national program designed by the US Sportsmen's
Alliance.  

The program is designed to provide children and their parents with
an introduction to outdoor sports along with the importance of
conservation through the mentoring of expert instructors.  The
events included archery, fishing, shooting, a demonstration of  
traditional firearms, traditional cooking, a demonstration of fox
hounds and the CT DEP Turn In Poacher Informatinal Trailer.

On behalf of US Sportsmen's Alliance, Bob Clark (CT Trailblazer
Event Coordinator) and all of the many volunteers and sponsors
who made this event possible, we would like to thank all of the
children who participated in hopes that some of these activities
play an important role in your life for years to come.
