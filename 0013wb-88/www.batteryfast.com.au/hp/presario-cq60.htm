    • How to Charge the Li-ion Hp presario cq60 battery?

    Claims of fast charging a Li-ion Hp presario cq60 laptop battery in one hour or less usually results in lower charge levels. Such a charger simply eliminates stage two and goes directly into 'ready' once the voltage threshold is reached at the end of stage one. The charge level at this point is about 70%. The topping charge typically takes twice as long as the initial charge.
    Depending on the charger and the self discharge of the Hp presario cq60 battery, a topping charge may be implemented once every 500 hours or 20 days. Typically, the charge kicks in when the open terminal voltage drops to 4.05 volts per cell and turns off when it reaches 4.20V/cell.
    No trickle charge is applied because the Li-ion laptop batteries for Hp presario cq60 laptop is unable to absorb overcharge. Trickle charge could cause plating of metallic lithium, a condition that makes the cell unstable. Instead, a brief topping charge is applied to compensate for the small amount of self-discharge the Hp presario cq60 battery and its protective circuit consume.
