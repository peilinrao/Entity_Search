Interlocking Brick

description

Wooden bricks, painted red on outer face, which interlock between rows by means of a bevelled tongue on the upper face and corresponding groove on the lower (see picture below). As well as various length bricks there are also angled gable bricks and printed card windows and doors. A folded card sheet provides the roof.
