[Normas para las notas] Notas del fotógrafo
Today being a National Day in Malta - St. Paul's Shipwreck, who brought us the Christian faith to our Island.
This is a B&W converted shot of the landmark of the Capital City. The dome belongs to the Basilica Church dedicated to Our Lady of Mount Carmel and the tall tower belongs to the Anglican Cathedral dedicated to St. Paul.
