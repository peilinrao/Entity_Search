Rentals
Rentals such as glassware, flatware, linens, tents, canopies, chairs,tables, party decorations and porta-bars are available at current rental prices. Rentals may be in limited supply during busy seasons. Buffalo Gals’ event managers can assist with determining proper amounts needed for your event. A 10% handling fee will be added to the final invoice. Client will be billed for lost or damaged items. Service staff is required in most instances (see below). A minimum of 72 hours notice is required on all cancellations.

Liquor
Please consult with our event managers about bar set-up. We can make arrangements with a variety of vendors for the delivery of beer, wine, liquor, mixes, ice, soft drinks, and all necessary supplies. We can assist in the planning, ordering, and delivery of alcohol to your event, but assume no liability or responsibility for the service of alcoholic beverages to minors or guests. Per Colorado liquor laws, you will be billed directly by the liquor vendor.

Serving staff
We have professional servers, bartenders, field chefs and grillers to assist with any type of event. The fee is $27.50 per hour for either a server captain, field chef, bartender or single server event. Grillers and each additional server is $25.00 after that. A 4-hour minimum is required for all servers, field chefs and grillers. Bartenders are required a minimum of 2-hours. For any event, final service staff hours may vary. Gratuity is not included.
