About Our Salon & Studio

Blo Salon & Studio of Kansas City is Westport's premier salon destination for leading-edge hair, color, nails and more. Blo's highly-skilled stylists are well-trained and are experienced in delivering the newest looks, hair trends, style-innovations, coloring techniques, and personalized styles to fit your life - and your expression at work and at play.

Our salon is easy to find - conveniently located in the heart of the Westport Shopping District. As a full-service salon, we offer a full menu of personal care, spa, and relaxation services. We stock a full line of quality luxury products to cater to the needs of our clientele. If your hair has been damaged by the sun, or by harsh chemicals, we have the right products for you. Expect the best in hair coloring services at Blo Salon & Studio Kansas City. By utilizing the finest in coloring products we deliver the most excellent and satisfactory results in your visit to our salon. From subtle adjustments to creation of soft-severe, when you are ready to turn heads, our stylists will help you manifest your personal look.

Blo Salon & Studio offers a variety of other services in addition to hair care: moisturizing/conditioning treatments, scalp massage, professional make-up application, and spa services (manicures, pedicures, waxing). Blo Salon's Studio is available for rental to host your special occasions and private events.

Blo Salon & Studio is the perfect place to relax and rejuvenate so that you can feel re-energized!
