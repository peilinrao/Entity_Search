  CATV Analyzer for Digital/Analog TV Transmission Networks

Perry Keller

Toronto, Canada - GAO RFID Asset Tracking (www.GAORFIDAssetTracking.com) recommends this RFID reader/writer which is very small yet full featured. It is designed for the desktop and has the unique ability to address both HF (13.56MHz) and LF (125kHz) RFID tags simultaneously. The high performance RFID read/write unit is widely used for the verification and programming of RFID tags and labels, personnel identification and access control to computers terminals or Point-of-Sales (POS) application, and many other applications for supply chain, retail, or logistics.
This portable RFID reader/writer, model 223015, has mouse-sized design making it easy to carry and convenient to use. The dual frequency is compatible with the following transponder protocols: ISO 156931-class unique number,1-Code™, 1-Code SLI, my-D, my-D secure, ISO 14443A, mifare®, ISO 14443B unique number in HF mode and HID®, proxEntry®, HITAG2, EM4002/4102 in LF mode. It can be connected to any standard USB port for data transmission as well as for power supply.This hot-plugable reader/writer reads/writes tags at distance up to 70mm. LED indicators verify the status of the RFID tag.
