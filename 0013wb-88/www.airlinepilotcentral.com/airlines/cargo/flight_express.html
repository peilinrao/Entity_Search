PILOT PAY (from company web site):
"Pilots make between $23,400 and $36,400 per year based on the run assigned. Pilots bid runs based on seniority, and the length of the duty day determines pay rate. All duty days of 7.5 hours or less pay $90 per day, and any time over and above 7.5 hours pays a $12.00 per hour of duty time, regardless of flight time. Example: A six-hour duty day pays the minimum of $75/day. Once your duty day goes to an eight-hour duty day your pay will be $12.00 a duty hour. So, a ten-hour duty day would pay you a $120.00."
